# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

A general audience, including hiring teams, potential collaborators, and anyone who wants to understand Deeksha's work without specialist knowledge. Visitors may arrive with little context and should quickly grasp what she does, how her responsibilities have grown, and what she is good at.

## Product Purpose

Present Deeksha as a calm, capable operations professional with a strong record in logistics, supply-chain coordination, and client relationships. The site should turn the most meaningful evidence from her CV into a concise professional story, make her progression easy to understand, and offer a simple path to contact her or read the full CV.

## Brand Personality

Calm, clear, and quietly confident. The voice is warm and direct, using everyday language rather than corporate jargon. The experience should feel soft and considered without becoming decorative or precious.

## Anti-references

Generic resume and CV templates, dense corporate profile pages, jargon-heavy business writing, over-designed portfolio sites, repetitive card grids, excessive animation, loud color, and decorative elements that compete with the content.

## Design Principles

- Distill to proof: keep only the details that help a visitor understand Deeksha's value.
- Plain language first: explain the work so a general audience can follow it immediately.
- Show progression: let increasing responsibility and measurable outcomes form the narrative.
- Quiet craft: use hierarchy, spacing, and typography to create confidence without visual noise.
- Keep the next step obvious: contacting Deeksha or opening her CV should never feel buried.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, focus visibility, semantic structure, keyboard use, and touch targets. Respect reduced-motion preferences, never rely on color alone, and keep line lengths and type sizes comfortable across phones, tablets, and desktops.
